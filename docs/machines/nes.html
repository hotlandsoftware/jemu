The Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) is an 8-bit third-generation video game console made by Nintendo, released in test markets in North America on October 18, 1985, with a full launch on September 27, 1986.
The NES was an extremely influential console which revitalized the global video game industry after the crash of 1983. It became a cultural phenomenon and sold over 62 million units worldwide.

A few notes about the current NES emulation state:

  • Mapper 0, 1, and 2 games work.
  • The emulator has mild graphical glitches but nothing is outright unplayable.
  • Only Standard Controller is supported in input as of now.
  • The AccuracyCoin Test scores a 17 / 140 - more than MarioNES (3), Nesticle (5), Famtasia (9), and JNes (14), but far less than iNES (67), Nezulator (71), No$NES (73), FCEUX (83), Nestopia (93), and NES Classic Edition (94).
